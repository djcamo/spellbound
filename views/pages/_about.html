About Spellbound

Spellbound Magical Gifts is a family owned and operated business located in the main street of the historic town of Gawler, about an hour north of Adelaide.  

Formerly known as "Fairy Secrets", Spellbound has been in the current location since 2001, and has been proudly supplying  the local & wider community with unique gifts and new age products for over 20 years. 

Spellbound staff take great care to provide a friendly personalised service, and value their loyal customers.
